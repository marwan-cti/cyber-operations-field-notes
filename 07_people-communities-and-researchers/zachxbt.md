# ZachXBT

**Type:** crypto investigator / OSINT / on-chain tracing

## Why he is on my radar

I discovered ZachXBT through crypto investigations, public threads about scams and major hack follow-ups. He is one of the figures who made me realize that crypto has its own OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) culture.

## What stuck with me

What interests me is the way he connects public blockchain data with off-chain clues: social media, exchange behavior, scam infrastructure, wallets, victims and public reporting.

It is a very modern investigator profile: fast, public, pseudonymous and community-facing.

## What I take from it

ZachXBT helped me understand that on-chain transparency does not equal automatic truth. Wallet flows are evidence, but they still need interpretation and caution.

That connects directly with my interest in CTI (Cyber Threat Intelligence): building a claim, showing evidence, and not over-attributing from one data point.

## Current limit

I still need more hands-on practice with blockchain analytics tools and wallet clustering.
