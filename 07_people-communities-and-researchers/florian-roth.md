# Florian Roth

**Type:** detection engineering / YARA / Sigma

## Why he is on my radar

I discovered Florian Roth through YARA, Sigma, GitHub repositories and detection engineering references shared by security practitioners.

## What stuck with me

What interests me is that his work sits at the point where CTI (Cyber Threat Intelligence) becomes useful to defenders. A report is one thing; a detection rule, hunting idea or repeatable check is another.

## What I take from it

Florian Roth helped me understand that detection is a craft. A rule is not just a string or a pattern. It carries assumptions, context, false positives and maintenance problems.

That matters for my path because I want to connect CTI with SOC (Security Operations Center) and threat hunting, not stay only in narrative analysis.

## Current limit

I still need to practice writing, testing and tuning YARA/Sigma rules myself.
