# Jason Haddix

**Type:** offensive security / bug bounty / AI red teaming

## Why he is on my radar

I discovered Jason Haddix through interviews, conference material and discussions around offensive security, recon, bug bounty and AI red teaming.

## What stuck with me

What interests me is his ability to turn offensive security into a method. It is not just “hacking energy”; it is recon, attack surface understanding, prioritization, communication and repeatable process.

His AI Security angle also caught my attention because he talks about AI systems practically, not only as abstract model-safety problems.

## What I take from it

Jason Haddix helps me connect attacker mindset with defensive thinking. For CTI (Cyber Threat Intelligence), understanding how an attacker sees exposure is as important as knowing actor names.

He also reinforces my interest in AI Security as a practical field around systems, tools, data and workflows.
