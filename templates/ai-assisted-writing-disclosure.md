# AI-assisted writing disclosure

This note may have been drafted, structured or edited with the help of AI tools such as ChatGPT or NotebookLM.

AI assistance may be used for:

- structuring rough notes;
- rewriting for clarity;
- extracting key questions;
- preparing Markdown formatting;
- generating checklists or templates.

Human review remains required for:

- source selection;
- source validation;
- factual accuracy;
- analytical confidence;
- caveats and uncertainty;
- final publication.

The objective is to use AI as an analytical assistant, not as a substitute for judgment.
