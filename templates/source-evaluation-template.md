# Source evaluation template

## Source

Title, author / organization, date and link.

## Type

Primary report, official advisory, vendor research, researcher blog, conference talk, podcast, journalism or social media pointer.

## Why it is useful

Explain what this source contributes.

## Limits / bias

Explain what the source may omit, exaggerate or frame in a specific way.

## Evidence level

- High: primary evidence, technical details, reproducible findings or official confirmation.
- Medium: reputable secondary reporting or well-supported analysis.
- Low: unsourced claim, social media pointer, rumor or weakly documented statement.

## How I will use it

Background context, technical evidence, timeline, attribution, defensive lesson or caveat.
