# Why crypto matters for CTI

Crypto matters for Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) because it connects technical compromise, financial infrastructure, laundering behavior, social engineering and geopolitical incentives.

## Key questions

- How do attackers gain access to wallets, bridges, private keys or internal systems?
- How do funds move after compromise?
- What can on-chain tracing prove, and what can it only suggest?
- How do independent investigators, exchanges, law enforcement and vendors interact?
- How do groups such as Lazarus use crypto theft as part of broader strategic activity?

## Defensive value

Crypto investigations help develop source evaluation, timeline reconstruction, attribution caution, OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) skills and awareness of financial threat infrastructure.
