# Ethics and boundaries

This portfolio studies offensive techniques, adversarial AI communities and cyber operations from a defensive, educational and CTI-oriented perspective.

It does not provide exploit instructions, operational abuse guidance, credential theft workflows, evasion procedures or step-by-step misuse guidance.

## Defensive intent

The purpose is to understand:

- how threats are discussed and documented;
- how incidents are contextualized;
- how threat actors operate at a high level;
- how defenders can improve detection, analysis and prioritization;
- how AI Security and CTI can help reduce risk.

## AI-assisted work

AI tools may help draft, structure or rewrite notes. Final responsibility remains human: source selection, source validation, caveats, confidence level and publication decisions.

## Handling sensitive topics

Topics such as jailbreak communities, intelligence-linked operations and crypto investigations are handled as objects of defensive study, not as endorsements.
