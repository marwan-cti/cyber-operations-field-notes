# About this portfolio

This portfolio is a public learning record focused on Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI), AI Security and defensive cyber analysis.

It is designed to show how I transform cyber culture into structured analysis: major incidents, threat actors, crypto investigations, supply chain attacks, AI red teaming communities, detection methods and public research.

## What this is

- A learning portfolio.
- A structured set of field notes.
- A place to connect cyber culture with defensive analysis.
- A way to document how my thinking evolves over time.

## What this is not

- It is not a collection of exploit guides.
- It is not an operational playbook for abuse.
- It is not a claim of senior expertise.
- It is not a replacement for primary sources or technical validation.

## Positioning

My current direction is junior CTI / SOC / AI Security. I am especially interested in the bridge between threat culture, adversarial behavior, defensive detection and intelligence analysis.
