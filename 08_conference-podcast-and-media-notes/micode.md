# Micode

**Type:** French cyber vulgarization / YouTube / internet investigations

## Why it is on my radar

I discovered Micode on YouTube as one of the main French-language gateways into cybersecurity, scams, hackers and internet investigations.

## What stuck with me

What I value is the way Micode makes cyber topics understandable and memorable. It is not deep CTI (Cyber Threat Intelligence), but it helped make the cyber world feel accessible before moving toward more technical sources.

## What I take from it

Micode reminds me that communication matters in cybersecurity. Being able to explain a technical or criminal story clearly is part of what makes cyber culture spread.

## Current limit

I treat Micode as a discovery source, not as final technical evidence.

## Resources I actually used

Micode YouTube videos and related French cyber/internet investigation content.
