# Conference, podcast and media notes

This section documents podcasts, YouTube channels and long-form media that shaped my cyber and tech culture.

The goal is not to treat media consumption as expertise. The goal is to show which sources helped me discover topics, people, incidents and concepts, and how I translate that exposure into more rigorous CTI (Cyber Threat Intelligence), SOC and AI Security learning.

## How to read this section

These notes are ecosystem notes, not final technical references.

A media source can be useful for:

- discovering a topic;
- understanding the human side of an incident;
- hearing practitioners explain their path;
- building motivation and curiosity;
- finding leads toward stronger primary sources.

But serious analysis still requires primary sources, vendor reports, official advisories, documentation, labs and independent validation.
